# Privacy Policy for YTIROK

**Last updated:** January 20, 2026

The **YTIROK** extension is designed to remind users to record video calls on Yandex.Telemost.

## Data Collection
We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, usage statistics, or other information to external servers. The extension operates entirely locally on your device.

## Permissions
- **Storage:** Used solely to save your local settings (e.g., reminder frequency).
- **Host Permissions (telemost.yandex.ru):** Used strictly to read the DOM structure of the active page to detect if a meeting is in progress and if the recording button is active.

## Contact
If you have any questions, you can contact the developer via the Chrome Web Store support page.
